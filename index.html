Pepyatka is the easiest way to share online

Pepyatka is an open source FriendFeed clone (yes, it is free and open!). Basically, this is a social real-time feed aggregator that allows you to share cute kittens, coordinate upcoming events, discuss any other cool stuff on the Internet or setup a private Pepyatka instance in your company.
